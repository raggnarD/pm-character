# Character Sprite Generation Prompts

These prompts are designed to generate character sprites in three distinct RPG styles, incorporating personality traits inspired by Ryan Reynolds, Sid from FF7, and an outdoorsy hunter/cowboy aesthetic.

---

## Prompt 1: 8-Bit Style Sprite

**For LLM Image Generation:**

```
Create a pixel art character sprite in classic 8-bit RPG style (similar to Final Fantasy I-VI or Chrono Trigger). The character should be:

CHARACTER DESIGN:
- A rugged, outdoorsy hunter/cowboy character with a charismatic, slightly sarcastic demeanor
- Wearing a weathered cowboy hat or wide-brimmed ranger hat
- Dressed in practical outdoor gear: leather vest, worn denim or canvas pants, sturdy boots
- Carrying hunting/survival equipment (rifle or crossbow on back, utility belt with pouches)
- Facial hair: light stubble or a well-groomed beard
- Confident, relaxed stance with a hint of swagger

PERSONALITY INFLUENCES:
- Ryan Reynolds energy: charming smirk, casual confidence, approachable but witty
- Sid from FF7 vibes: mechanical/technical elements (goggles on hat, tool pouches, gadgets)
- Outdoorsy hunter: rugged, self-reliant, connection to nature

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
- Resolution: 128x128 to 256x256 pixels
- Color palette: Limited to 16-32 colors maximum (authentic 8-bit aesthetic)
- Style: Sharp pixel art with clear outlines, no anti-aliasing
- Pose: Standing idle pose, front-facing or 3/4 view
- Background: Transparent or simple solid color
- Animation-ready: Design should work for walk cycles and basic animations

COLOR SCHEME:
- Earth tones: browns, tans, forest greens
- Accent colors: dusty blue (for denim), metallic gray (for tools/weapons)
- Warm skin tones appropriate for pixel art
- Pops of color for personality (red bandana, brass belt buckle)

STYLE REFERENCES:
- Final Fantasy IV-VI character sprites
- Chrono Trigger character design
- Secret of Mana pixel art quality
```

## Prompt 2: FF7 Original (PSX) Style Sprite

**For LLM Image Generation:**

```
Create a character sprite in the style of Final Fantasy VII (1997 PlayStation) pre-rendered 3D character models. The character should be:

CHARACTER DESIGN:
- A charismatic outdoorsman/hunter with cowboy influences and mechanical aptitude
- Wearing a distinctive cowboy/ranger hat with goggles or tech accessories attached
- Outfit: Rugged leather jacket or vest over a casual shirt, cargo pants with utility pockets
- Accessories: Tool belt with mechanical gadgets, ammunition pouches, survival gear
- Weapon: Modern hunting rifle or futuristic crossbow strapped to back
- Facial features: Strong jawline, confident expression with a hint of smirk, light facial hair
- Build: Athletic and capable, not overly muscular

PERSONALITY FUSION:
- Ryan Reynolds charm: Relaxed posture, approachable expression, subtle humor in pose
- Sid from FF7 influence: Mechanical elements (wrench in belt, tech goggles, pilot/engineer aesthetic)
- Hunter/outdoorsman: Weathered clothing, practical gear, connection to wilderness

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
- Resolution: 256x256 to 512x512 pixels
- Style: Low-poly 3D model aesthetic with pre-rendered look
- Texture quality: Slightly pixelated/dithered textures (authentic PSX era)
- Lighting: Soft ambient lighting with slight rim light
- Pose: Confident standing pose, slight contrapposto, one hand near belt
- Background: Transparent PNG
- Color depth: 16-bit color palette (65,536 colors) with slight banding

COLOR PALETTE:
- Primary: Earth tones (brown leather, tan canvas, olive green)
- Secondary: Denim blue, metallic silver/gunmetal
- Accents: Warm brass, deep red (bandana or accent piece)
- Skin: Natural tones with subtle shading
- Mako influence: Subtle green glow on one tech gadget (nod to FF7 aesthetic)

STYLE REFERENCES:
- FF7 character models (Barret, Cid, Vincent quality)
- Pre-rendered 3D with painterly textures
- Slightly chunky proportions (authentic PSX character design)
- Expressive despite low poly count
```

## Prompt 3: FF7 Rebirth (Modern) Style Sprite

**For LLM Image Generation:**

```
Create a high-resolution character portrait/sprite in the style of Final Fantasy VII Rebirth (2024) - modern, detailed, semi-realistic anime aesthetic with exceptional quality. The character should be:

CHARACTER DESIGN:
- A rugged, charismatic hunter/outdoorsman with cowboy and engineer influences
- Age: Late 20s to mid-30s, weathered but youthful
- Facial features: Strong, defined features with Ryan Reynolds-like charm - expressive eyes, confident smirk, well-groomed stubble or short beard
- Hair: Tousled, slightly messy (windswept), medium length, natural brown with sun-bleached highlights
- Hat: High-quality leather cowboy/ranger hat with mechanical modifications (goggles, small tech display, weathered texture)

OUTFIT DETAILS:
- Layered clothing: Weathered leather jacket (with technical modifications) over a casual henley or work shirt
- Pants: Tactical cargo pants or reinforced denim with visible wear and tear
- Boots: High-quality leather hiking/riding boots with metal accents
- Accessories: Detailed tool belt with visible tools, ammunition, and gadgets; fingerless gloves; bandana or scarf

EQUIPMENT:
- Primary weapon: Sleek, modern hunting rifle with custom modifications (visible on back or held casually)
- Secondary: Mechanical gadgets, survival knife, tech device (inspired by Sid's engineering)
- Visible details: Scratches, wear patterns, personalized modifications

PERSONALITY & POSE:
- Expression: Confident, slightly cocky smirk (Ryan Reynolds energy)
- Eyes: Warm, intelligent, hint of mischief
- Posture: Relaxed but capable, leaning slightly or with weight on one leg
- Hand position: One hand near weapon or tool, other relaxed or adjusting hat
- Overall vibe: "I've got this handled, and I'll probably make a joke about it"

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
- Resolution: 1024x1024 pixels minimum (high detail)
- Style: Modern semi-realistic anime (FF7 Rebirth quality)
- Rendering: Physically-based rendering (PBR) with realistic materials
- Lighting: Dramatic three-point lighting with warm key light, cool rim light
- Textures: Highly detailed - visible fabric weave, leather grain, metal scratches
- Background: Transparent or subtle environmental blur (outdoor/workshop setting)
- Format: PNG with alpha channel

COLOR GRADING:
- Overall tone: Warm, earthy, slightly desaturated for rugged feel
- Primary colors: Rich browns (leather), warm tans, forest greens
- Accent colors: Deep blue (denim), brass/copper (buckles, bullets), steel gray (tools, weapon)
- Skin: Natural, sun-kissed tones with realistic subsurface scattering
- Eyes: Warm brown or hazel with catchlights
- Special effect: Subtle mako-green glow on one tech gadget (FF7 signature)

ARTISTIC INFLUENCES:
- FF7 Rebirth character design (Cloud, Barret, Cid level of detail)
- Tetsuya Nomura's realistic character art
- Western RPG influences (Red Dead Redemption aesthetic meets FF7)
- Modern anime realism (high-quality CG anime style)

MOOD & ATMOSPHERE:
- Confident and capable
- Approachable despite ruggedness
- Blend of old-west charm and futuristic tech
- Character who's equally comfortable in wilderness and workshop
```

## Usage Notes

1. **Input Image**: When using these prompts, provide a reference image of the person whose likeness should be incorporated
2. **Customization**: Adjust specific details (clothing colors, accessories) based on your preferences
3. **Iteration**: These prompts work best with iterative refinement - generate, review, and adjust
4. **Consistency**: Use the same reference image across all three styles for character consistency
5. **Animation**: For 8-bit and FF7 styles, consider requesting multiple poses for animation frames

## Recommended AI Tools

- **8-Bit Style**: Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion with pixel art LoRA
- **FF7 Original**: Midjourney or Stable Diffusion with retro 3D/PSX aesthetic models
- **FF7 Rebirth**: Midjourney v6+, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion XL with anime/realistic models
